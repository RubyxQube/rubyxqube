## Recommended Aesthetic Direction for `/frontend-design`

**Suggested direction:** Clean, warm, and approachable — think "trusted neighbor, not a franchise." Light and airy with a hint of warmth. Crisp white or warm off-white backgrounds, bold before/after imagery front-and-center, and a personality-forward layout that lets the name and Scott's reputation do the heavy lifting. Not corporate, not cold. The vibe of a really clean home on a sunny Idaho morning.

**Why:** Scott's entire competitive advantage is personal trust built through word-of-mouth and Nextdoor. The site should feel like an extension of that — a real person with a funny name who does meticulous work, not a franchise landing page. Clean, light, and confidence-inspiring without being sterile.

**Accent color direction:** Deep sky blue or teal with a bright white base and warm cream/tan secondary. Evokes cleanliness, freshness, and Idaho's open-sky feel. Avoid the generic "cleaning company green" that every competitor uses. A pop of a warm amber or gold for CTAs adds warmth and makes it feel personal rather than clinical.
