## Recommended Aesthetic Direction for `/frontend-design`

**Suggested direction:**
> Raw industrial concrete. Heavy, textured, built-to-last. Think poured slabs, rebar, the honest beauty of a perfectly finished driveway. Dark background — charcoal or deep slate — with bright white type and a single bold accent (burnt orange or steel blue). Asymmetric layout. Big bold headline. No fluff.

**Why:** Matches the material itself — concrete is honest, heavy, permanent. The aesthetic should feel like the work: no shortcuts, no decoration for its own sake. Speaks directly to the Mountain Home market (blue-collar, practical, military-adjacent).

**Accent color direction:** Burnt orange (`#c2612a`) or steel blue (`#3a6b8a`) — both feel rugged and regional, neither is generic contractor green.
