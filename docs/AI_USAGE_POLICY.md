# AI Receptionist — Client Usage Policy

> Share this with clients during onboarding alongside their service agreement.  
> Plain language. No legal jargon.

---

## What the AI Receptionist Is

The AI receptionist is a chat assistant trained specifically on your business — your services, pricing, service area, and frequently asked questions. It lives on your website and is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

It is built on large language model technology (the same class of AI as ChatGPT). It is not a pre-scripted chatbot — it understands natural language, handles questions it wasn't explicitly trained on, and gives accurate, context-aware answers about your business.

---

## What It Does

- Greets visitors and offers to help
- Answers questions about your services, pricing, and service area
- Asks qualifying questions to understand what the visitor needs
- Collects their name, phone number (or email), and what they're looking for
- Sends you an SMS notification the moment a lead is captured
- Works while you're on a job, asleep, or unavailable

---

## What It Does Not Do

- Make binding quotes or commitments on your behalf
- Access your schedule, calendar, or internal systems (unless booking integration is added)
- Take payments
- Speak for you in disputes or legal matters
- Retain conversation history between separate sessions

---

## Conversation Limits

Your plan includes **unlimited AI conversations**. There are no per-chat fees and no monthly conversation caps for standard business usage.

**Fair use:** The plan is designed for typical small service business websites. If your site experiences unusually high traffic (e.g., a viral post driving thousands of visitors in a short period), we'll reach out to discuss options before any change is made. In practice, this threshold is far above what most service business sites generate.

---

## What Data Is Collected From Your Customers

When a visitor interacts with your AI receptionist, the following may be collected:

| Data | When collected |
|------|---------------|
| Messages sent in the chat | Every conversation |
| Name | When visitor provides it voluntarily |
| Phone number or email | When visitor provides it voluntarily |
| Service requested | When visitor describes their need |

This data is:
- Sent to you via SMS notification when a lead is captured
- Stored securely for up to 24 months
- Never sold or shared with third parties
- Available for deletion upon your request

---

## Your Responsibilities

As the business owner, you are responsible for:

1. **Having a privacy policy on your site** that discloses the AI chat and lead collection. We provide a template and help you set this up during onboarding.
2. **Keeping your business information current.** If your services, pricing, or service area changes, tell us and we update the AI within 2 business days.
3. **Not using the AI to misrepresent your business.** The AI says what you tell us to tell it. Accurate information in = accurate answers out.

---

## Updating the AI

Your AI is not static. As your business changes, we update it:

- **Service/pricing changes:** Tell us via text or email. Updated within 2 business days.
- **New FAQs:** If customers keep asking the same question the AI isn't handling well, we add it.
- **Seasonal changes:** Hours, availability, seasonal services — just let us know.

Updates to the AI are included in your monthly plan. There is no per-update fee.

---

## Accuracy & Limitations

The AI is trained on information you provide. It will:
- Answer questions it has been trained on accurately
- Make reasonable inferences for questions adjacent to its training
- Acknowledge when it doesn't know something and offer to connect the visitor with you

It will not:
- Invent services, prices, or policies you haven't told us about
- Guarantee specific outcomes or results
- Replace your judgment on complex, unusual, or sensitive customer situations

**Recommendation:** After launch, test your AI yourself. Ask it 10 questions a customer would ask. Tell us what it got wrong and we'll fix it.

---

## If Something Goes Wrong

If your AI gives a visitor inaccurate information:
1. Contact us immediately via text or email
2. We'll identify what caused the issue and correct it
3. If needed, we'll reach out to the affected visitor on your behalf

We monitor AI performance as part of monthly care. If we notice patterns in incorrect responses, we proactively update the training.
