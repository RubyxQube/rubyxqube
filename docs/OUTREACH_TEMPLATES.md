# Cold Outreach Templates

Templates for cold email, SMS, and DM outreach to local business prospects.

**Rules:**
- No em-dashes. Ever. Use a regular hyphen ( - ) or rewrite the sentence.
- 4 sentences max on email. 2-3 on text/DM.
- Always reference something specific the researcher found.
- One link. One ask.

---

## Cold Email

**Subject line options (pick one):**
- `I built something for [Business Name]`
- `[Business Name] - quick question`
- `Your website, rebuilt`

**Body:**

```
Hey [Name],

I noticed [specific pain point - e.g., "you have 47 Google reviews but your website doesn't show any of them"]. I spent a couple hours building what I think your site could look like:

[demo URL]

No strings attached. If you like the direction, I'd love to chat for 15 minutes. If not, feel free to keep it as inspiration.

Boyd
(208) 970-8624
```

**Swap the pain point based on what the researcher found:**
- Bad/no site: "your current site hasn't been updated in a while and isn't showing up on mobile"
- No reviews shown: "you have [X] Google reviews but your website doesn't feature a single one"
- No chatbot/lead capture: "your site has no way to capture leads after hours"
- GBP gaps: "your Google listing is missing [hours/photos/services] which is probably costing you calls"
- Spending on ads: "you're running Google Ads but the landing page isn't set up to convert"

## Follow-Up SMS (3-4 days after email, no response)

```
Hey [Name], Boyd here. Sent you an email a few days ago with a rebuilt version of your website - wanted to make sure it didn't get buried. [demo URL] - worth a quick look?
```

If they reply with interest but go quiet:

```
No rush at all. Happy to jump on a 15-minute call whenever works for you. Just reply here and we'll find a time.
```

## Instagram / Facebook DM

**First message (cold):**

```
Hey [Name] - I build websites for [trade] businesses in the Treasure Valley. I put together a version of what I think your site could look like. Mind if I send you the link?
```

Wait for a "sure" or positive reply before sending the link. Sending the link cold on DM looks spammy.

**After they say yes:**

```
Here it is: [demo URL]

Built it specifically for [Business Name] - services, your area, everything. There's also an AI receptionist in the corner that answers questions and captures leads 24/7. Let me know what you think.
```

---

## Google Business Profile Message

Almost nobody cold outreaches through GBP. Low competition, high visibility.

```
Hi, I'm Boyd - I build websites for local [trade] businesses in [city]. I put together a demo of what a new site could look like for [Business Name]. Happy to share the link if you're curious.
```

---

## General Notes

- Send from boyd@rubyxqube.com - not a generic info@ address
- Plain text email only - no HTML, no images, no banners. Looks more personal, avoids spam filters.
- Follow up once. Then move on. Two touchpoints max.
- If they open the email 3+ times without replying, send the SMS version.
- Best days to send: Tuesday and Wednesday morning. Avoid Monday and Friday.
