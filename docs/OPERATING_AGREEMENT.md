## OPERATING AGREEMENT OF RUBYXQUBE LLC

This Operating Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **May 2026**, by **Boyd Isaac Querubin** ("Member"), the sole member of RubyxQube LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Idaho.

---

## Article 1 — Organization

**1.1 Name.** The name of the Company is **RubyxQube LLC**.

**1.2 Formation.** The Company was formed upon the filing of its Articles of Organization with the Idaho Secretary of State in accordance with the Idaho Limited Liability Company Act (Idaho Code § 30-25-101 et seq.).

**1.3 Principal Office.** The principal place of business is located in **Boise, Idaho**. The Member may change the principal office location at any time.

**1.4 Registered Agent.** The Company shall maintain a registered agent in Idaho as required by law. The registered agent may be changed by filing the appropriate form with the Idaho Secretary of State.

**1.5 Purpose.** The Company is organized to engage in any lawful business activity, including but not limited to web design, software development, AI integration services, and digital marketing services.

**1.6 Term.** The Company shall continue indefinitely unless dissolved in accordance with this Agreement or applicable law.

---

## Article 2 — Member

**2.1 Sole Member.** The Company has one member:

| Member | Address | Ownership |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Boyd Isaac Querubin | Boise, Idaho | 100% |

**2.2 Additional Members.** No additional members may be admitted without the written consent of the existing Member.

---

## Article 3 — Management

**3.1 Member-Managed.** The Company shall be managed by its Member. The Member has full and exclusive authority to manage, operate, and control the business and affairs of the Company.

**3.2 Authority.** The Member is authorized to:
- Open and manage bank accounts on behalf of the Company
- Enter into contracts and agreements
- Hire employees and contractors
- Make all financial and operational decisions
- Execute documents in the name of the Company

**3.3 Officers.** The Member may appoint officers (including themselves) with titles such as President, CEO, or Manager. Boyd Isaac Querubin is hereby designated as **Owner and Manager** of the Company.

---

## Article 4 — Capital Contributions

**4.1 Initial Contribution.** The Member has contributed capital to the Company in the form of cash, services, and/or property as agreed upon by the Member.

**4.2 Additional Contributions.** The Member may, but is not required to, make additional capital contributions at any time.

**4.3 No Interest.** No interest shall accrue on capital contributions.

---

## Article 5 — Distributions and Profits

**5.1 Distributions.** The Member may take distributions from the Company at any time and in any amount, provided the Company can pay its debts as they come due.

**5.2 Allocation.** All profits, losses, and distributions are allocated 100% to the Member.

---

## Article 6 — Taxation

**6.1 Tax Treatment.** As a single-member LLC, the Company shall be treated as a **disregarded entity** for federal and state income tax purposes. All income and losses shall be reported on the Member's personal tax return (Schedule C or applicable form).

**6.2 EIN.** The Company has applied for / obtained an Employer Identification Number (EIN) from the Internal Revenue Service.

---

## Article 7 — Bank Accounts

**7.1 Business Account.** The Company shall maintain one or more business bank accounts separate from the Member's personal accounts. Only Company funds shall be deposited into and disbursed from business accounts.

**7.2 Authorized Signatories.** Boyd Isaac Querubin is authorized to open, manage, and transact on all Company bank accounts.

---

## Article 8 — Liability

**8.1 Limited Liability.** The Member shall not be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company, except as required by Idaho law.

**8.2 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify the Member against claims arising from acts taken in good faith on behalf of the Company.

---

## Article 9 — Dissolution

**9.1 Dissolution Events.** The Company shall be dissolved upon:
- Written decision of the Member to dissolve
- Operation of law

**9.2 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Member shall wind up the Company's affairs, pay all debts, and distribute remaining assets to the Member.

---

## Article 10 — Miscellaneous

**10.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Idaho.

**10.2 Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended at any time by the Member in writing.

**10.3 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.

**10.4 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire operating agreement of the Company and supersedes all prior discussions or agreements.
