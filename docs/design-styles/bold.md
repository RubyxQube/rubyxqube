# The Bold — Build Guidelines

> **Best for:** Auto Repair · Towing · Moving · Pest Control · Security · Gym · Locksmith · Appliance Repair

---

## Core Philosophy

Every visitor is one call away from becoming a job. High energy, zero friction, maximum urgency. This site exists to get the phone ringing. Speed of communication beats everything else.

---

## Color System

| Role       | Default Value | Notes |
|------------|--------------|-------|
| Background | `#ffffff`    | White — clean and fast-loading feel |
| Navbar     | `#1e40af`    | Strong blue — authority and reliability |
| Hero bg    | `#1e40af`    | Blue hero — commanding |
| Accent     | `#f97316`    | Orange — maximum contrast CTAs |
| Text       | `#1e293b`    | Dark on light sections |
| Muted      | `#94a3b8`    | Secondary text, fine print |
| Cards      | `#f1f5f9`    | Light cards on white |

Client palette may use red/black for urgency, or navy/gold for security companies. Orange accent can become red or yellow. Keep the contrast brutal.

---

## Typography

- **Headings:** Heavy (800). No apologies. "We Fix It. Today."
- **Hero H1:** 52–64px. Direct statement. Solve the problem in 5 words.
- **Body:** 16px, generous line-height. Clear and scannable.
- **Use ALL CAPS sparingly** for urgency badges — "SAME-DAY SERVICE" / "AVAILABLE 24/7"

---

## Layout Structure (Top to Bottom)

### 1. Navbar
- Blue background, white text, orange CTA button
- Logo left, minimal nav (2–3 links max — people don't browse, they call)
- Phone number prominently in the nav — tap-to-call on mobile
- Optional: thin urgency bar above nav — "🟢 Available Now — Call (xxx) xxx-xxxx"

### 2. Hero Section
- Blue background, full width
- Bold white headline: "Boise's Fastest Auto Repair."
- Subhead: "Same-day appointments. Most repairs done while you wait."
- TWO CTAs side by side: "(208) 970-8624 — Call Now" (orange) + "Get a Quote" (outline)
- Optional: counter/badge — "⚡ 847 jobs completed this year"
- No hero image needed — the copy IS the hero

### 3. Urgency / Trust Bar
- White background, 4–5 icons in a row
- ⚡ Same-Day Service | 🔧 Licensed Techs | 💰 Upfront Pricing | ⭐ 4.8 Stars | 📍 Serving [City]

### 4. Services Grid
- 3-column grid, white cards on light gray background
- Bold service name, 1-line description, price range or "Call for quote"
- Each card: icon + name + brief detail + "Book This →" link
- Keep service names punchy: "Engine Diagnostics" not "Comprehensive Powertrain Evaluation"

### 5. Why Us / Key Differentiators
- Blue or orange background band — break up the white
- 3–4 bullet points with icons: what makes them better than the competitor down the street
- "We answer the phone. We show up on time. We quote before we start."

### 6. Reviews
- 4–5 Google reviews
- Customer name, star rating, short punchy quote
- "See all [X] reviews" link
- Real reviews from real names convert for this audience

### 7. FAQ (optional but great for auto, pest, moving)
- "How much does [service] cost?" / "How long does [service] take?" / "Do you offer guarantees?"
- Short direct answers. No marketing speak.

### 8. Contact / Booking Section
- Orange background or bold blue CTA band
- Phone number MASSIVE: 60–72px
- Short form: Name + Phone + Service needed + "Contact me"
- "Or text us at (xxx) xxx-xxxx" if applicable

### 9. Footer
- Dark footer, simple
- Business name, phone, address, hours
- Emergency/after-hours contact if they offer it
- Service area list

---

## Mobile Rules
- Phone number in nav must be a `tel:` link — non-negotiable
- CTA buttons full-width on mobile
- Orange CTAs always visible — don't hide them below fold
- Keep hero text short enough to read without scrolling on mobile

---

## Content to Collect from Client
- [ ] Business name, owner name, phone, email
- [ ] Services + pricing (even if just ranges)
- [ ] Hours of operation + emergency hours if applicable
- [ ] Service area
- [ ] Years in business
- [ ] License/certification numbers (auto, pest control require this)
- [ ] 4–6 Google reviews
- [ ] Unique selling points (fast turnaround, warranty, flat pricing, etc.)

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Don't make this pretty — make it functional and urgent
- Don't add too many nav links — people don't browse, they scan for the phone number
- Don't bury the phone number — it goes in nav, hero, AND footer minimum
- Don't use pastel colors or soft typography — this audience needs strong visual signals
